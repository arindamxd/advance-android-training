Hundreds of thousands of public records from government organizations and private-sector companies
were consulted for this report, including 45 government organizations; these were broken down into
1,271 sub-units. Some 1,931 private companies were identified that engage in top-secret work for
the government. For each company listed, employee data, revenue, and date of establishment were
obtained from public filings, Dun & Bradstreet data, and original reporting.
